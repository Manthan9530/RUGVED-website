Remote Unmanned Ground Vehicular Electronic Defence Systems

About Us

R.U.G.V.E.D is a student-led defense tech initiative focused on creating Remote Unmanned Ground Vehicular Electronic Defence systems. Our mission is to bridge the gap between innovation and application in military-grade automation, AI, robotics, and electronics.
